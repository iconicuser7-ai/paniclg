About the College

A Legacy of Academic
& Technical Excellence

Panimalar Engineering College, located in Poonamallee, Chennai, is a premier institution dedicated to producing technically competent, ethically grounded, and industry-ready engineers.

Affiliated with Anna University and approved by AICTE, the college offers a range of undergraduate and postgraduate programmes designed to meet the demands of a rapidly evolving technological landscape.

Our commitment is to cultivate not just engineers but innovators — individuals who lead with knowledge, solve with creativity, and contribute meaningfully to society.

With experienced faculty, state-of-the-art laboratories, and robust industry partnerships, Panimalar Engineering College provides an environment where potential is nurtured into achievement.

Where knowledge meets innovation and ambition meets opportunity.
